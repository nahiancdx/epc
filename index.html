Who We Are

We've Been Finding Bargains for Over 30 Years

Every Penny Counts is a discount superstore based in Buckley, North Wales. For over three decades, we've been buying end-of-line, surplus, and clearance goods from some of the UK's largest mail order and retail companies.

Because retailers need to clear old stock to make way for new lines, we get exceptional deals — and we pass every penny of those savings on to you. Top brand names at prices you won't find on the high street.
